## 2024-04-20 - Pre-calculate squared distances in high-frequency animation loops
**Learning:** In performance-critical Three.js animation loops like `js/background.js`, performing repeated multiplications (e.g., `mouseDistance * mouseDistance`) inside inner loops with thousands of iterations (`particlesCount = 2000`) introduces unnecessary overhead. Pre-calculating these squared threshold constants outside the loop provides a free and clean micro-optimization without sacrificing readability.
**Action:** When optimizing performance-critical loops containing geometric distance calculations, always derive and pre-calculate squared threshold constants (e.g., `mouseDistanceSq`) during initialization or immediately after configuration declaration to avoid redundant computation per frame.
