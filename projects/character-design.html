Character Design

Character modeling, rigging and animation for interactive experiences

Project Overview

This character design project focuses on creating believable digital characters that combine elements of traditional Chinese folklore with contemporary design sensibilities. The character was designed for an interactive narrative experience where player choices influence character development.

The design process began with extensive research into mythological figures and their symbolic representations in Chinese culture. These elements were then reinterpreted through a modern lens to create a character that feels both authentic to its cultural roots and relevant to contemporary audiences.

Special attention was paid to the character's emotional expressiveness, with a facial rig capable of conveying a wide range of subtle emotions. The character's movements were designed to reflect their personality and backstory, with animation sets that evolve as the character develops through the narrative.

Project Details

Technologies

  • Blender for Modeling
  • ZBrush for Sculpting
  • Substance Painter for Texturing
  • Maya for Rigging and Animation
  • Marvelous Designer for Cloth Simulation

Character Stats

  • Polygon Count: 35,000 (high-poly) / 15,000 (game-ready)
  • Bone Count: 120 (including 45 facial bones)
  • Animation Sets: 42 unique animations

Technical Challenges

Dynamic Clothing System

Created a dynamic clothing system that realistically responds to character movement and environmental factors like wind. The multi-layered garments maintain collision integrity while preserving the traditional aesthetic of the design.

Advanced Facial System

Developed a blend shape-based facial animation system with over 60 unique expressions. The system includes a procedural component that simulates micro-expressions and realistic eye movement to enhance character believability.
