01 — About

About Me

I am a Game Designer currently pursuing my Master of Art in Digital Art and Humanities at the University of British Columbia. My work explores the boundary where interactive media meets critical scholarship.

With a BFA in Interactive Design and Game Development from SCAD, and professional experience across Unreal Engine, Unity, WebGL, and shader development, I create interactive experiences that merge artistic vision with technical craft.

My research interests include game-based education, game design theory, and the intersection of media archaeology.
